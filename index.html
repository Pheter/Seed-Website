A minimal REST framework for PHP. Grow beautiful web apps from a tiny framework!

Seed is a simple framework for developing RESTful PHP web applications. By focusing on the principles of REST and encouraging you to develop your app around the concept of resources, you are rewarded with a simple and logical application structure. The result is maintainable and less bug-prone code that is both easier to read and write.

The framework is highly flexible due to a powerful plugin system. You are free to use any templating engine, ORM, or library you wish. This allows you to tailor the framework to your preferences, and the needs of the project resulting in quick and efficient development.
